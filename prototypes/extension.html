How the Rezco Chrome Extension Works

Inspired by Otter.ai's side-panel pattern. Reads the DOM directly. PMs clip what they're reading without changing behavior.

1

PM browses

Bloomberg, Gmail, IR website, sell-side PDF — anywhere relevant.

2

Click Rezco icon

Side panel slides in from the right. No tab switch needed.

3

DOM read

Detects email vs. webpage vs. PDF. Extracts structured content via JavaScript.

4

Auto-tag

First-pass enrichment: companies, themes, sectors surfaced for confirmation.

5

POST to Go service

Payload sent to Rezco's Go ingestion microservice.

6

Indexed

Pushed to Vertex AI Search, filed in Drive, linked to entities. Atlas can query immediately.
